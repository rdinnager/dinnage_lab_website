Research Visualization Gallery

Our research creates compelling visualizations that help communicate complex biological patterns and processes. This gallery showcases some highlights from our work.

Morphological Evolution in Latent Space

These visualizations demonstrate how our deep learning models capture and manipulate complex biological forms.

Beetle Morphology Exploration

This animation shows the exploration of beetle morphology in the latent space of a deep generative model. By navigating this learned space, we can generate hypothetical morphologies and understand the underlying structure of morphological variation.

Bird Bill Evolution

This visualization demonstrates how our models capture the evolution of bird bill shapes across a phylogeny. The ability to model complex 3D shapes in a biologically meaningful way opens new possibilities for studying the relationship between form and function across evolutionary history.

Species Distribution and Environmental Modeling

Phylogenetic Random Fields

This figure represents different realizations of spatial random fields constrained by phylogenetic relationships. These models allow us to incorporate evolutionary history into predictions of species distributions and community assembly.

Biodiversity Visualization

This mosaic visualizes phylogenetic relationships among species using silhouettes from PhyloPic. Approaches like this help communicate complex evolutionary relationships to both scientific and public audiences.

NicheFlow: A Foundation Model for Ecology

These visualizations are from our NSF-funded work on NicheFlow, a foundation model for ecological niche modeling.

Latent Space of Environmental Variables

This plot visualizes the reduced-dimension latent space learned by our model of bioclimatic variables, revealing the intrinsic structure of environmental gradients globally.

High-Throughput Phenomic Analysis

PhenoVision Detection Results

Example results from the PhenoVision framework showing automated detection of plant reproductive structures in field photographs. The system achieves exceptional accuracy while processing millions of images.

Eco-Evolutionary Model Inference

Phylogenetic Branch Regression

Visualization of results from our Phylogenetic Branch Regression method (implemented in the fibre R package), showing how trait evolution is distributed across branches of a phylogenetic tree.

Simulation Results

Visualization of results from population genomic simulations using our slimr R package, showing how genetic variation changes across space and time under different demographic scenarios.
